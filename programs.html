Programs

Access funded across sports, ages, and communities.

COAF funds access to youth and adult athletics through multiple programs. Each one delivers our mission to a different community — and your support reaches all of them.

Three programs. One mission.

Youth · Volleyball · Partner program

Colorado Boom

Competitive and developmental youth volleyball in the Denver metro. COAF scholarship funding helps cover club fees and training for players whose families need support.

About youth volleyball
Youth · Basketball · Supported program

Team EVO

Year-round basketball development for young athletes — personalized coaching that builds basketball IQ and fundamentals. COAF funding opens access for underserved players.

About youth basketball
Adults · Fitness · COAF program

COAF Community Fitness

Our own adult-access program: affordable fitness and athletics for adults, with a focus on women and minority communities, run at our Aurora facility.

About community fitness

Colorado Boom and Team EVO are independent organizations supported by COAF through documented scholarship and access funding. COAF Community Fitness is the foundation's own adult program. In every case, the foundation's role is the same: making sure cost is never the reason someone can't participate.
