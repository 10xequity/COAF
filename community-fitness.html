Adults · COAF program

COAF Community Fitness.

The foundation's own adult-access program: affordable fitness, training, and athletics for adults in our community — with a focus on opening doors for women and minority participants.

The program

Healthy communities don't age out at 18

Most access programs stop at youth sports — but the cost barriers that keep kids off teams keep adults out of gyms, leagues, and training too. Gym memberships, class fees, and league dues add up fast, and the adults priced out are disproportionately women and members of minority communities.

COAF Community Fitness is our answer: low-cost and scholarship-supported fitness and athletics programming for adults, run at our Aurora facility at 14200 E Alameda Ave. Sessions focus on conditioning, skills, and community — a welcoming on-ramp whether you're returning to fitness or starting for the first time.

Like everything we do, the program exists to deliver our mission: accessibility, diversity, and opportunity. Everyone is welcome.

Apply for program access Ask about sessions
Any participation fees are kept intentionally low and are reinvested directly into funding the foundation's mission and scholarship pool.

What the program offers

Built around the people it serves

Group fitness & conditioning

Accessible strength and conditioning sessions for all fitness levels, led in a supportive, judgment-free environment.

Women's access focus

Dedicated programming and funded spots that prioritize women who face cost or access barriers to fitness and athletics.

Community athletics

Recreational sport sessions that build community alongside fitness — because belonging is half the benefit of athletics.
