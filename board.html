Board & governance

The people accountable for the mission.

COAF is led by two volunteer boards working toward one goal — keeping athletics accessible. An operations board runs the foundation; a fundraising board builds the partnerships and resources that fund the programs.

Operations board

Running the foundation

The operations board governs finances, scholarship awards, compliance, and day-to-day stewardship of every dollar raised.

Governance

How we govern

Oversight & awards

The operations board meets regularly to review finances, approve scholarship awards, and evaluate program partnerships. Scholarship decisions are made by the board based on demonstrated need and available funds — never by any partner program.

Two boards, one mission

Separating operations from fundraising keeps stewardship and revenue-building focused and accountable. The fundraising board grows the resources; the operations board governs how they're used — both serving the same charitable mission.
