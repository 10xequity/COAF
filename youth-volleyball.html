Youth volleyball · Partner program

Youth volleyball with Colorado Boom.

COAF funds scholarships and access for young volleyball athletes through our partnership with Colorado Boom, an independent youth volleyball club in the Denver metro area.

The program

Competitive volleyball, accessible to every family

Club volleyball builds elite skills, lifelong friendships, and pathways to high-school and college play — but club fees, gear, and travel routinely cost families thousands of dollars per season. That price tag is where talented kids quietly disappear from the sport.

Through our partnership with Colorado Boom, COAF directs scholarship funding to players who need it: covering club dues, training costs, and equipment so a family's budget never decides a kid's roster spot.

Colorado Boom is a separate organization with its own coaching staff, teams, and operations. COAF's role is access — we fund opportunities for athletes who couldn't otherwise participate.

Apply for a volleyball scholarship Visit Colorado Boom (partner site)
Scholarship awards are made by COAF based on financial need. Program operations, team placement, and coaching are handled by Colorado Boom.

What COAF funding covers

Removing every cost barrier we can

Club & registration fees

Full or partial coverage of seasonal club dues and tournament registration for athletes with demonstrated need.

Training & development

Skills clinics, conditioning, and off-season training so funded athletes develop on the same footing as their teammates.

Gear & equipment

Shoes, knee pads, and uniforms — the up-front costs that stop families before the season even starts.
