Where the money goes

Scholarships & access

The core of our spending: need-based awards covering fees, training, and gear across youth volleyball, youth basketball, and adult community fitness.

Programming

Direct delivery of COAF Community Fitness sessions and community athletic events that serve and grow participation.

Keeping the lights on

A lean share goes to the basics every charity needs — insurance, facilities time, and administration — kept as low as we can make it.

Want detail before you give? See our Impact page or contact the board. We report how donated dollars are used — the impact of your specific gift — rather than publishing our full operating books. You can verify our charitable status (EIN 93-1548121) through the IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search.
